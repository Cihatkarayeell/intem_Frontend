ABOUT INTEM

Intem was
founded in 1993
as a family business in İstanbul, where a dedicated team set out to provide luxury customer experience through our services and pioneer within the knitwear production. 
For over 31 years,
design, quality and expertise of craftsmanship
remain at the heart of what we do, committed to sustainable practices.
Our corporate values, rich knowhow and innovative approach led us to become one of the leading knitwear manufacturer in the industry for the international high-street brands. 
We are
offering fully integrated production service from yarn to garment
, operating in 55.000 m2 total production area  located in İstanbul, Malatya and Düzce facilities where we meticulously oversee every step of the production processes to ensure high quality standards. 
With a legacy built on trust and excellence, delivering exceptional products that embody our passion for our business and customer satisfaction

PROJECTS & NEW INVESTMENTS

For over 31 years, as one of the leading knitwear company in Turkey,
innovation
has become one of our core codes that symbolizes our commitment
for constant improvement & change
within our business. This corporate approach leds us going further to create better solutions for our customers. We persistently invest in new technologies and projects to be always one step ahead. Today at
Intem
at many stages both in managerial systems and manufacturing processes, we have achieved a noticeable
digitalization
. We are currently working on 3 projects:
LIVING & TALKING FACTORIES
Thoughtful manufacturing and design processes for sustainable future Real-Time data integration of transparency
MES PRODUCTION SYSTEM IMPLEMENTATION
Leveraging data through MES will make a great impact on providing valuable insights for the optimizations of our operations.
AI ASSISTANT
Developing our AI assistant KNIT-o to provide real-time data access for all our internal departments, to enhance our reporting and information chain.

PHILOSOPHY

We believe true success is rooted in the power of collective growth. Putting effort do the best of our practices to become a little better constantly and executing with care&sincerity to create value for people and customers is the key to be ahead. Our journey is driven by the collaboration of our customers. Together, we create knitwear that reflects shared values.For us, true success is not just individual achievements, but about growing together to build a lasting legacy in the industry.
“Collective Growth Is The Universal Code For A Meaningful Success”
