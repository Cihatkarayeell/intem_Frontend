KNITWEAR PRODUCTION

At İntem, we take immense pride in our commitment to high-quality knitwear production.
Our philosophy revolves around complex craftsmanship, and excellence of quality.
Quality control is an integral part of our production process. Each garment undergoes a series of
internal inspections
, from initial design to final production,
ensuring that every detail is perfect.
With a continuous improvement, we actively adapt our processes to maintain our position as leader in the knitwear industry.
We operate in fully integrated facilities
with a production capacity
exceeding 30K units per day,
enabling us to meet the fast respond to short lead time demands 6-8 weeks with our
skilled employees
who bring years of expertise to every piece consist of
over 800.
We export our products mainly European market, establishing strong
partnership with renowned global fashion brands.

YARN PRODUCTION

As pioneers in the fancy yarn industry, we develop

elevate fashionable products

transform ordinary yarn into fashionable materials.
